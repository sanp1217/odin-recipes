Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes

A Side Dish That Can Go With Any Main Dish This Holiday!

This simple and easy to make dish will have you craving for more!

Ingredients For 1 Serving

  • 1 medium potatoe, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1/3 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of shredded Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a casserole dish.
  2. Arrange sliced potatoes in the casserole dish; season with salt
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until a paste forms. Pour in milk gradually, stirring constantly, until a thick white sauce forms, about 5 minutes. Stir in cheese and remove from heat. Pour sauce over the potatoes.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are cooked through and tops are lightly browned, about 1 hour.
